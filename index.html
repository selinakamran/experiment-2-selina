Zodiac Academy


Darcy Vega

Darcy is a main character of the Zodiac Academy book Series. She's the daughter of Hail ad Merissa Vega, the former king and queen of Solaria. She has a twin sister named Tory, and a brother named Gabriel. She falls in love with Orion, which they later find out they are Elysian Mated.

Tory Vega

Tory is a main character of the Zodiac Academy book series. She's the daughter of Hail ad Merissa Vega, the former king and queen of Solaria. She has a twin sister named Darcy, and a brother named Gabriel. She falls in love with Darius, an heir to the thrown, their story is an enemies to lovers genre.

Darius and Orion

Orion is the former Professor at Zodiac Academy. He gets fired after the dean and student body learns of his relationship with Darcy. He takes the blame and goes to Darkmore penitentiary. Later in the series we learn that the two are Elysian Mated (soulmates choosen by the Stars).
Darius is one of four Heirs of the Solarian Throne. He refuses his attraction with Tory and continually ridicules her infront of everyone causing them to become enemies. Darius refuses to bow to the True Queens, Darcy and Tory, but later in the Series after he is brought back to life from the dead by Tory, he bows to her infront of all of Solaria.
